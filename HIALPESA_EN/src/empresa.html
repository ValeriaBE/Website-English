About us

Manufacturing yams as well as knit fabric and garments.

Our vertical operation allows us to have total control over quality, the advantage of coordinating product development, starting with the thread and most importantly, the ability to offer our customers flexibility and versatility times through Short deliveries and transparency in the supply chain. We constantly strive to improve and implement procedures to keep us competitive in the market international. We believe in the need to build strong ties with our customers. The The main reason for our success story is our commitment to our customers.



CERTIFICATIONS

Hialpesa is Control Union certified for organic cotton. We are certified for our spinning, knitting, dyeing & manufacturing divisions. We hold OE 100 certification. Control Union will certify our product whether it is blended organic yarns or 100% organic yarns. WRAP certification is an internationally known factory certification for responsible business practices. WRAP ensures our customers know they are working with a business partner that adheres to local & international labor standards.

SOCIAL COMMITMENT

En Hialpesa valoramos otras cosas más que sólo la empresa. Tenemos programas que apoyan el bienestar de nuestros empleados, como programas médicos y apoyo económico especial a nuestros trabajadores que sufren imprevistos urgentes.

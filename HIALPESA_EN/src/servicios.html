Processes

Manufacturing yarns as well as knit fabric and garments.

Fibers


We primarily work with cotton fiber, both long and extra-long staple. Peruvian cotton is ranked among the finest cottons in the world. We also offer heathers and blends and develop these as needed for our customers. We usually spin blend with polyster, viscose, alpaca, rayon, among others.

A focus of ours is Organic Cotton. Organic cotton is grown, picked and ginned according to organic standards which prohibits the of pesticides and protects the soil and farmers. HIALPESA was one of the first suppliers to offer organic cotton and today we are one of the largest exporters of organic garments in Peru. Our selection includes long staple Organic Pima. We are one of the only countries to offer both of these fiber lengths in organic.

GO GREEN!

HIALPESA WAS ONE OF THE FIRST SUPPLIERS TO OFFER ORGANIC COTTON.

Spinning


CAPACITY: 250,000 KG PER MONTH
COUNTS: ROM 6/1 TO 60/1
PRODUCTS: CARDED RINGSPUNG, COMBED RINGSPUNG OR OPEN END IN HEATHERS, HIGH TWIST, BLENDS, SLUB YARNS AND MULTI-PLY YARNS.

Our spinning mill was set up in 1979. With continuous investment, we now have 43,500 ring spinning spindles, in addition to rotors for open end spinning at our disposal. We are currently producing 250,000 kilograms of yarn monthly. Our mill is capable of manufacturing various count ranging from 6/1 to 60/1 with an ability to ply yarns when needed.

Knitting


“IN THE PURSUIT OF MAINTAINING OUR HIGH LEVEL OF SERVICE WE ARE CONTINUOSLY STRIVING TO IMPROVE AND UPDATE OUR MACHINERY, TECHNOLOGY AND THE SKILLS OF OUR PEOPLE”

Our knitting division has the capacity to produce 220,000 kilograms of fabric per month. We work 100% cotton and blended fabrics and can knit Jersey, Interlocks, Ribs, Fleece, Pique, Pointelle, Mini Jacquards, Ottomans, French Terry, etc…. in solid and yarn dye. All our technicians and workers are consistently trained and updated. Our team of engineers are highly qualified and continuously researching, enabling us to create new trends.

WE HAVE THE CAPACITY TO PRODUCE 220,000 KG PER MONTH.

Dyeing & Finishing


NEW EQUIPMENT, HIGH QUALITY CHEMICALS AND DYES, AND OUR VERY WELL TRAINED STAFF ALLOW US TO OFFER TO OUR CUSTOMERS EXCELLENT RESULTS

The dyeing division has modern machines to ensure the quality dyeing with Azo-free dyes. There are several quality control checks to ensure uniform dyeing and maintain minimum shrinkage controls. The plant, equipped with Soft Flow Dyeing Tumble Dryer, produces 360,000 kilograms of dyed fabric per month. The entire dyeing process is monitored by quality control managers of the company.

All our fabric can be finished either tubular or open with depending on the article. We have both a tubular and an open with compacting machine which enable us to control shrinkage. We have a laundry with different washes and garment dyes. Our product range includes direct, relative and pigment garment dyeing in a variety of finishes at our customer’s request.

Manufacturing


STRENGTHS QUICK RESPONSE, COMPETITIVES PRICES, ON TIME DELIVERY, DUTY FREE-USA&EUROPE, CLEAR COMMUNICATION, DESIGN CAPABILITY.

MANUFACTURING FLEXIBILITY
REASONABLE STYLE AND GRAPHICS MINIMUMS SHORT LEADS TIMES.

We have the capacity to produce 550,000 pieces of cut and sew knit garments every month. Our product range of knit garments includes t-shirts, polo, shirts, hooded sweatshirts, pants and shorts for men, ladies and children. We are fully vertical, including embroidery and screen printing services in house. Our sampling team is capable of developing garments as per your styles and specifications. Our experienced and creative personnel will work with you from the beginning of the development process to help you get the product you want.

Our garment manufacturing team prides themselves on the quality control procedures that they have in place at every stage of the garment from fabric, to patterns, to sewing to final audits. Our quality control measures are fully supported by test reports on shrinkage, color fastness, color match, light fastness, test on accessories, yarn, thread and stitch quality. Apart from in-house testing, third party accredited laboratories are employed and certificates are obtained pertaining to each test. We control 100% of the garments and ensure that we only ship first quality units to our customers.

WE HAVE THE CAPACITY TO PRODUCE 550,000 OF CUT AND SEW KNIT GARMENTS EVERY MONTH.

Finishing and Packaging


Our strict quality and packaging process ensures that our customers only receive garments of optimum quality

Cutting


Our specialized staff and software turn the cutting routine into a level of very high precision that allows the fluidity of the other processes quickly. The molds are worked by modelers with enough experience to follow strictly the instructions of our customers in both forms and measures.

Sewing


Our sewing facility has about 1,500 specialized employees able to produce high quality garments, with sufficient machinery to handle seams such as flatseammer, invisible sewing, berths, remalles, coated, necks among others that they allow us to work any type of garments from the most basic to the most complex. Our staff is in constant training and evaluation.

Quality Control of Garments


In addition to the controls at each point of the process, we have a final control where 100% of all garments are audited, thereby ensuring that our customers Only receive fully approved production.

Finishing and Packaging


We have steaming machines (no vaporization) that are constantly renewed to avoid any staining of the garments and only ensure products completely ironed and in correct measurements.

HIALPESA was established in 197, since then we have climbed to greater heights of success. Now after thirty years of dedicated service to our clients, we can proudly say that they have come to associate the name of HIALPESA with quality, service and innovation. Today, we are one of the largest vertically integrated mills in Peru.

We specialize in manufacturing yams as well as knit fabric garments. We are one of the few fully vertical exporters in Peru which enables us to offer competitive prices. Our vertical operation also enables us to have total control over quality, the advantage of coordinating product development starting from yam and most importantly, the ability to offer our customers short lead-times and transparency down the supply chain. We strive to continually improve and implement procedures to stay competitive in the international marketplace. We believe in building strong relationships with our customers. The major reason begin our success story is our commitment to our clients.
